# ImproveIt.Today — 3-Minute Trailer Scenario (18 × 10s scenes)

**Format:** 3:00 exactly — 18 scenes, 10 seconds each. One scene = one generated clip.
**Why 10s:** current AI video tools (Kling 2.x, Veo 3, Runway Gen-4) produce native ~10s clips —
this scenario maps 1:1 onto the generation pipeline in `trailer-production-guide.md`
(keyframe → animate → assemble). Use the same Style Anchor and Character Sheet from that guide.

**Color grammar (never break it):** AMBER = a problem seen. GREEN = a problem solved.
**Structure:** The World → The One → The World, Changed. (whole → one → whole)

---

## ACT 1 — THE WORLD (scenes 1–4, 0:00–0:40)

### SCENE 1 · 0:00–0:10 — "The Blue Marble"
- **VISUAL:** Black. A single point of blue light grows — Earth from deep space, whole, borderless, turning slowly. Terminator line sweeps day into night.
- **CAMERA:** Slow push-in from black, barely perceptible drift.
- **AUDIO:** Near-silence. Low resonant hum. One piano note at 0:07.
- **VO:** —
- **TEXT (lower third, small):** *From up here, everything looks fine.*
- **COLOR STATE:** Neutral / blue.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Earth from deep space, photorealistic, no labels or borders, terminator line visible, deep black background, single faint star field, cinematic 2.39:1.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Extremely slow planetary rotation, gentle camera drift forward, no cuts.

### SCENE 2 · 0:10–0:20 — "The Descent"
- **VISUAL:** The camera falls toward Earth — fast. Cloud layers whip past; the curve of the horizon flattens as we plunge.
- **CAMERA:** Vertical dive, accelerating, slight roll.
- **AUDIO:** The hum swells; a heartbeat rhythm enters underneath. Wind builds.
- **VO:** *"Seven billion people."*
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** Neutral → first amber pinpricks visible far below at frame end.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** High-altitude atmospheric dive toward a night continent, cloud layers streaking past camera, faint amber city lights below, cinematic.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Fast forward plunge through clouds, motion blur, subtle camera shake, lights below growing.

### SCENE 3 · 0:20–0:30 — "A Billion Flickers"
- **VISUAL:** Above a darkened continent: tiny amber points of light blink on. Dozens → hundreds → thousands, each a flicker, not a glow — like embers.
- **CAMERA:** Slow aerial glide, tilting down.
- **AUDIO:** Heartbeat steadies. Strings enter, unresolved.
- **VO:** *"Every one of them walks past something — every single day — that could be better."*
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** AMBER ignition — the motif is born here.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Aerial night view of a sprawling city, thousands of tiny amber pinpoint lights flickering across districts like embers, dark blue night grade, cinematic.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Lights multiplying steadily across the frame, slow forward glide, no cuts.

### SCENE 4 · 0:30–0:40 — "What We Walk Past" (montage: 3 sub-shots ≈3s each)
- **VISUAL:** Street level, dusk, three hard cuts:
  **(a)** a deep pothole filling with rain, cars swerving;
  **(b)** a flooded crossing, a student stepping over on bricks;
  **(c)** a dead streetlight over a black crosswalk.
- **CAMERA:** Static, locked-off frames — problems don't move.
- **AUDIO:** Real-world ambience only — rain, traffic, a distant siren. Music holds its breath.
- **VO:** —
- **TEXT (one word per sub-shot):** *Seen.* / *Seen.* / *Ignored.*
- **COLOR STATE:** Each problem carries a faint amber rim-light.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPTS:** (a) rain-filled pothole close-up on asphalt at dusk, amber streetlight reflection; (b) flooded pedestrian crossing, person balancing on bricks, dusk; (c) unlit streetlight looming over a dark crosswalk, silhouetted.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Minimal motion — rain ripples, one passing car, flickering distant lights.

## ACT 2 — THE ONE (scenes 5–13, 0:40–2:10)

### SCENE 5 · 0:40–0:50 — "Maria"
- **VISUAL:** The dark crosswalk from 4(c). MARIA (40s, tired, real — not a model) stops at the corner holding her kid's hand. She looks up at the dead light. She has looked up at it a hundred times.
- **CAMERA:** Slow push-in from across the street to a medium close-up.
- **AUDIO:** Music drops to the heartbeat alone. Kid's footsteps.
- **VO:** —
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** Amber rim on the dead lamp.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Tired woman in her 40s holding a child's hand at a dark street corner at dusk, looking up at an unlit streetlight, documentary realism, shallow depth of field.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Slow dolly-in, her breath visible, kid fidgets, she keeps looking up.

### SCENE 6 · 0:50–1:00 — "One Report"
- **VISUAL:** Insert: her phone. Camera app → snap → a soft UI chime; a pin drops on a map. Cut wide: above her head, a single amber point of light blinks on — same amber as orbit.
- **CAMERA:** Macro on phone → whip to wide low-angle.
- **AUDIO:** The chime is the first "designed" sound in the film. Heartbeat warms.
- **VO:** *"What if the broken thing everyone sees — and no one fixes —"*
- **TEXT:** *One report.*
- **COLOR STATE:** First personal amber light ignites.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Close-up of a phone photographing a dark streetlight at dusk, map pin UI glowing amber; then wide shot of woman and child under a single floating amber point of light.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Phone shutter, UI pin drop animation, light blinks on and hovers, gentle pulse.

### SCENE 7 · 1:00–1:10 — "The Vote"
- **VISUAL:** Nairobi, morning. A teenager on a packed matatu upvotes a flooded-road report. With each tap of ⬆ across many hands/screens (3 quick inserts), Maria's kind of light — everywhere — brightens a step.
- **CAMERA:** Handheld energy, life everywhere.
- **AUDIO:** Percussion joins the heartbeat — momentum begins.
- **VO:** *"— had somewhere to go?"*
- **TEXT:** *Vote.* (appears as a clean word, bottom left)
- **COLOR STATE:** Amber lights brightening.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Kenyan teenager on a crowded minibus tapping an upvote arrow on a phone showing a flooded road photo, morning light, documentary realism.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Thumb taps, upvote count ticks, cut to city lights pulsing brighter.

### SCENE 8 · 1:10–1:20 — "The Pool"
- **VISUAL:** Lisbon evening, neighbors on a stoop passing one phone around. €5. €10. €20. A funding bar climbs toward a goal. Laughter. Someone's grandmother adds €2 and gets a cheer.
- **CAMERA:** Warm close orbits around the group.
- **AUDIO:** Music warms — a human, communal texture (hand claps, low brass).
- **VO:** *"What if a neighborhood could put its money where its problems are?"*
- **TEXT:** *Fund.*
- **COLOR STATE:** The amber light over their street grows from point to glow.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Neighbors of mixed ages on a Lisbon stoop at golden hour passing a phone, a crowdfunding progress bar visible on screen, warm and communal, documentary realism.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Phone passed hand to hand, progress bar filling, group leans in and cheers.

### SCENE 9 · 1:20–1:30 — "The Desk"
- **VISUAL:** A city operations office at night. A public-works officer's dashboard pings — *threshold reached*. A district map with amber clusters; one cluster pulses urgently. She leans in, drags it to "ACKNOWLEDGED."
- **CAMERA:** Over-the-shoulder onto the screen, then her face in monitor glow.
- **AUDIO:** A single clean alert tone over the building score.
- **VO:** *"What if the people responsible... could finally see?"*
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** First amber→green transition begins (the acknowledged cluster turns green-rimmed).
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** City official at a dark operations desk, large monitor showing a district map with amber problem clusters and a threshold alert, face lit by screen glow.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Alert pulse on screen, she leans forward, drags a card across a board UI.

### SCENE 10 · 1:30–1:40 — "The Solver"
- **VISUAL:** Lisbon, dawn. A retired electrician reads a job notification at his kitchen table: *"Streetlight repair — 2km away — funded."* He nods once. Picks up a worn tool bag by the door.
- **CAMERA:** Still frames, dignity in the ordinary. Door closes behind him.
- **AUDIO:** Music pulls back for one bar — one man's decision. Then returns fuller.
- **VO:** *"And what if fixing it... was somebody's best day of work?"*
- **TEXT:** *Solve.*
- **COLOR STATE:** His route on the map draws a thin green line toward an amber point.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Retired electrician at a modest kitchen table at dawn reading a phone notification, weathered hands, worn leather tool bag by the door, warm documentary realism.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** He reads, nods, stands; hand lifts the tool bag; door light spills in.

### SCENE 11 · 1:40–1:50 — "The Bid"
- **VISUAL:** Split rhythm: bids appearing under Maria's streetlight report (amounts, timelines, ratings) → one bid card flips to **ACCEPTED** → at the corner, a work glove shakes Maria's hand.
- **CAMERA:** UI inserts intercut with the human handshake — end on the hands.
- **AUDIO:** Score gathers — strings begin to climb. A soft "accepted" chime.
- **VO:** *"Not a complaint into the void. A contract with your street."*
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** Maria's amber light gains a green ring — work agreed.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Phone UI showing competing repair bids with ratings, one card stamped accepted; then close-up of a work glove shaking a woman's hand on a street corner.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Bid cards slide in, accepted stamp lands, cut to firm handshake, slight camera settle.

### SCENE 12 · 1:50–2:00 — "The Work"
- **VISUAL:** Day. The electrician on a ladder inside the streetlight's open panel — sparks, focus, competence. Below, Maria's kid watches from across the street, chin up, transfixed.
- **CAMERA:** Low angle up the ladder (hero framing for ordinary work); insert of hands on wiring.
- **AUDIO:** Real work sounds kept high in the mix — ratchet, wire snip. Score holds underneath.
- **VO:** —
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** The amber point above the corner pulses faster — anticipation.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Electrician on a ladder repairing an open streetlight panel in daylight, sparks, child watching from across the street, hopeful documentary realism.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Hands working in the panel, one spark, kid tilts head up, light breeze.

### SCENE 13 · 2:00–2:10 — "Light On"
- **VISUAL:** Dusk again — same framing as Scene 5, deliberately. The streetlight flickers... then **blazes to life**. Maria and her kid walk under it, lit, safe. Her phone: a green checkmark. Above: the amber point **turns green**.
- **CAMERA:** The Scene-5 push-in, reversed — we pull back as light floods the crosswalk.
- **AUDIO:** The score **resolves** for the first time. The chime from Scene 6 returns, one octave up.
- **VO:** *"One light."*
- **TEXT:** *Resolved.*
- **COLOR STATE:** **THE TURN** — first full amber→green on screen.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Streetlight bursting to life at dusk over a crosswalk, mother and child walking beneath in warm light, green glow motif, cinematic and emotional.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Flicker then full illumination, pool of light spreads, two figures cross through it.

## ACT 3 — THE WORLD, CHANGED (scenes 14–18, 2:10–3:00)

### SCENE 14 · 2:10–2:20 — "The Chain Reaction"
- **VISUAL:** The map, top-down: Maria's green point flares — and around it, amber points turn green one after another, spreading block by block like rain starting.
- **CAMERA:** Slow rise off the map, city blocks resolving into light patterns.
- **AUDIO:** Each turn is a soft note — the turns become the melody.
- **VO:** *"One problem solved doesn't stay one problem."*
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** Green cascade begins.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Top-down stylized city map at night, one bright green light triggering neighboring amber lights to turn green in a spreading wave.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Radial cascade of amber-to-green conversions, gentle camera rise.

### SCENE 15 · 2:20–2:30 — "Everywhere" (montage: 3 sub-shots ≈3s each)
- **VISUAL:** **(a)** the Nairobi road — drained, kids cycling through where the flood was; **(b)** the Lisbon stoop under a new lamp, the neighbors' street glowing; **(c)** the city officer's district map — clusters mostly green, she allows a small smile.
- **CAMERA:** The same framings as scenes 7/8/9 — paid off.
- **AUDIO:** Score at full stride now — strings, brass, the heartbeat as a drum.
- **VO:** *"It becomes proof."*
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** Green overtaking amber in every frame.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPTS:** Mirror scenes 7/8/9 with the problem fixed and green light grading.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Joyful, ordinary motion — cycling kids, neighbors toasting, dashboard turning green.

### SCENE 16 · 2:30–2:40 — "The Ascent"
- **VISUAL:** We rise — street, rooftops, clouds — the reverse of Scene 2. Below, the city glitters, amber turning green in waves, like sunrise moving across a map.
- **CAMERA:** Accelerating vertical rise, clouds part around us.
- **AUDIO:** The opening hum returns — but **major key, resolved**. Full score.
- **VO:** *"Change the world? No."*
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** Green spreading past the horizon.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Ascending aerial shot at night leaving a city where waves of amber lights turn green across districts, clouds parting, hopeful cinematic grade.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Continuous accelerating ascent, green wavefront expanding below.

### SCENE 17 · 2:40–2:50 — "Seven Billion Corners"
- **VISUAL:** Earth full-frame again — the Scene 1 shot, but alive: points of green blooming across the night side of every continent, spreading like dawn.
- **CAMERA:** The same slow drift as Scene 1 — the bookend.
- **AUDIO:** Score peaks, then strips back to the piano and the resolved hum.
- **VO:** *"Change your corner of it. Seven billion corners... is the world."*
- **TEXT:** —
- **COLOR STATE:** The planet, healing in green light.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Earth from deep space at night, continents scattered with green points of light spreading like sunrise, photorealistic, hopeful, cinematic 2.39:1.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Slow rotation, green lights multiplying gently, no cuts.

### SCENE 18 · 2:50–3:00 — "Logo / CTA"
- **VISUAL:** The green lights converge and resolve into the ImproveIt.Today logo on black. Clean. Held.
- **CAMERA:** Static.
- **AUDIO:** One final piano note — the same note as 0:07, resolved. Silence. Cut to black.
- **VO:** —
- **TEXT (in sequence, 2s apart):**
  1. **Report. Vote. Fund. Solve.**
  2. **ImproveIt.Today**
  3. *Your world. Starting now.*
- **COLOR STATE:** Green → white logo on black.
- **KEYFRAME PROMPT:** Minimal logo reveal, hundreds of green light points flowing into a clean wordmark on pure black.
- **MOTION PROMPT:** Particle convergence into logo, settle, hold 3 seconds.

## ASSEMBLY NOTES

- **Timeline math:** 18 clips × 10.0s = 3:00 exactly. Cut hard on the 10s marks for scenes 1–3 and 16–18 (the "cosmic" scenes want clean cuts); allow ±0.5s breathing on the human scenes (5–13) and trim into the montages (4, 15) — they absorb slack.
- **The two montage scenes (4 and 15)** are each generated as three ~3.5s clips and cut internally — every other scene is one continuous 10s generation.
- **VO record:** one session, one narrator — warm, grounded, documentary. The full VO script reads in ~55 seconds; it must *never* feel rushed — silence is part of the grammar.
- **Sound bookends:** Scene 1's unresolved hum and piano note = Scene 17/18's resolved hum and note. Same sounds, different keys. This is the emotional spine.
- **Framings pay off:** Scene 13 reuses Scene 5's framing (reversed camera). Scene 15 reuses 7/8/9's framings. Scene 17 reuses Scene 1. The audience feels the rhyme even if they don't notice it.
- **Production pipeline:** follow `trailer-production-guide.md` — Style Anchor on every keyframe prompt, Character Sheet for Maria/the electrician/the teenager, keyframe → image-to-video per scene, then the 5-layer stack (clips → VO → music → SFX → text).
